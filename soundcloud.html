A proposed alternative way of music discovery experience for SoundCloud through leveraging the social aspect.

OVERVIEW

As a audio-streaming and sharing platform, SoundCloud allows users to be both listeners and creators, fostering the diverse creation of niche genres and music that is original to SoundCloud. Despite cultivating such a diverse large selection of music, SoundCloud’s songs recommendation often falls under HipHop and Rap, which limits exposure for other genres. We saw this as a missed opportunity to appeal to its own niche audience. Jump to solution.

Therefore for our 6-weeks senior experience design project at SFU, through extensive case studies and ideation, we proposed an additional way of discovery on top of what SoundCloud is currently doing by leveraging the social aspect that is unique to the brand.

GUIDING PRINCIPLE

To ground our project so it caters specifically to our client, we identified SoundCloud’s Brand Pillars that helps to align our designs to their values.

The Social Aspect

During the field research of music industry, we noticed that only SoundCloud is offering a wide range of social aspect from commenting on specific timestamp in the music, reposting musics, and direct message which allows discussion to happen in platform.

Hidden Gems

With everyone having the equal and easy access to upload their music to SoundCloud, the platform became a place to discover new and pioneering music from all genres. With the current system and algorithm, SoundCloud is creating friction by burying the niche genres under bigger tracks, making hidden gems literally hidden.

SoundCloud Problem Space

Framing Question

How might we increase the likelihood for hidden gems to be discovered?

RESEARCH

Through our initial research, we found out that since SoundCloud’s peak of 150 million registered users in 2015, they have lost 49% of their users by 2020. This was caused by the imbalance of what the users are interested in listening and engaging to, which consists more of niche music genres, and what the platform had been continuously surfacing which falls under Hip-Hop and Rap from well known musicians.

SoundCloud has struggled with the relevance and precision of their recommendations and many have departed from the platform due to this kind of ‘musical Russian roulette’.

– Jennie Silber, Music Recommendation Algorithms: Discovering Weekly or Discovering Weakly?

Through 8 of our user interviews with current SoundCloud users and artist, we narrowed down our target audience into SoundCloud listeners who aren’t there in the platform for bigger music genres, but wanted to explore more smaller tracks and genre. We also identified common listening behaviours for music enthusiasts through secondary research. In which we concluded into three major behaviours.

SoundCloud Extend Discovery Demo

SOUNDCLOUD EXTENDED DISCOVERY

Two new ways of music discovery in SoundCloud that extends on SoundCloud's current music search. This feature emphasizes user-to-user recommendations and an audio-focused browsing experience. We intentionally designed these features to be desktop-first as based on our user interviews, all participants search new music inspiration through the web version in their desktop.

AN AUDIO-FIRST BROWSING

Users are able to hover through tracks to hear a preview of the most-engagement section. Song details will appear after hovering longer than two seconds, providing more options to explore. On top of that, we added microinteractions in the song details such as the filter tag, and direct link to the user's profile based on the songs that were pulled in; extending more discovery pathways.
